Understanding TKPH

What is TKPH?

TKPH (Ton-Kilometers Per Hour) is a critical metric for tire performance monitoring. It represents the work a tire performs based on the load it carries and the distance it travels per hour.

TKPH Formula:

TKPH = Average Speed (km/h) × Average Load (tons)

Every tire has a specific TKPH rating assigned by the manufacturer. Exceeding this rating leads to heat buildup, accelerated wear, and potential failure. Our system helps you stay within safe operating limits.

TKPH Risk Levels

Safe Zone (0-150)

Normal operation, minimal wear, optimal tire life

Warning Zone (151-300)

Increased wear, monitor closely, potential for early replacement

Danger Zone (>300)

High risk of failure, excessive heat buildup, immediate action required
